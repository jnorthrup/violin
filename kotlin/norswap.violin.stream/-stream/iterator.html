iterator

open operator fun iterator(): Iterator<T>

Converts the stream to an iterator.

The stream and the iterator are linked: items consumed within one will not show up in the other.

This method ensures that streams can be used in Kotlin for-loops. This is why it isnt called toIterator, and why it is not an extension method (to make for loops available without the need to import extension methods).
